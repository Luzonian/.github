# The Luzonian

> *Ask Questions. Demand Answers.*

**The Luzonian** is the official collegiate student publication of the **Manuel S. Enverga University Foundation (MSEUF) – Lucena City**, Quezon, Philippines. As the voice of the MSEUF student body, The Luzonian is committed to delivering truthful, accurate, and impactful journalism that serves the campus community and beyond.

---

## 🏛️ About Us

The Luzonian takes its name from the **Luzonian Colleges** — the institution founded by Dr. Manuel S. Enverga on January 14, 1947 — which would eventually grow into the Manuel S. Enverga University Foundation. The publication stands as a proud legacy of that founding spirit: a dedication to education, service, and the pursuit of truth.

We operate under the **Department of Arts, English, and Literature (DAEL)** and serve as the primary campus journalism arm of MSEUF's collegiate student body.

---

## 🎯 Vision

To establish The Luzonian as a formidable vessel of truth and integrity — a community that fosters a reliable environment and empowers readers to think critically and engage meaningfully with the world around them.

## 📌 Mission

To inform, inspire, and empower readers by delivering truthful and captivating information. We place journalistic integrity and the unbiased truth above all else, championing accuracy in every story we tell.

## 🤝 Contributing

The Luzonian is composed of student editors, writers, photographers, illustrators, and designers from MSEUF. If you are an MSEUF student interested in joining the publication or submitting work, reach out through our official social media channels or visit the DAEL department.

For event coverage requests, fill out our coverage request form at least **three (3) business days** prior to your event.

## 📜 Publication History
